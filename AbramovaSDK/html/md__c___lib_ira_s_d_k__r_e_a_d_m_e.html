Cartoon Chuck`s journey

Author Abramova Irina

This cartoon was made of absolutely unsuitable objects which were done while procedure studying. So I think, it to be more arthouse rather than classic.

Annotation

The main character this little story - yellow bird, called Chuck. He rides a skateboard watching all around at ease. Than he travels down the river, been surrounded by very picturesque pink mountains. At the end, following traditional behavior of Angry Birds, he flies. But does small harm as usual.

>Animation was done by using special graphic library "TXLib.h". >The author of library Dedinskiy Ilya. TXLib.h This short animation includes more then twenty objects which have opportunity to be animated.
